About bb.q Chicken

Established in 1995 as part of parent company Genesis BBQ, bb.q Chicken is a globally recognized Korean fried chicken brand with a mission to provide the highest quality and most delicious chicken in the world. Rooted in the belief that food is a gateway to culture, bb.q Chicken delivers Korea's Finest Fried Chicken™ and authentic K-Food experiences at each location.

The brand name "bb.q" stands for "Best of the Best Quality," reflecting its commitment to premium ingredients, consistent quality, and innovation. bb.q Chicken has rapidly expanded across North America and beyond, earning recognition as one of the fastest-growing restaurant chains in the U.S., as well as being named among Yelp's "Most Loved Brands."
