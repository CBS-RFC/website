About bb.q Chicken

Established in 1995 as part of parent company Genesis BBQ, bb.q Chicken is a globally recognized Korean fried chicken brand with a mission to provide the highest quality and most delicious chicken in the world. Rooted in the belief that food is a gateway to culture, bb.q Chicken delivers Korea's Finest Fried Chicken™ and authentic K-Food experiences at each location.

The brand name "bb.q" stands for "Best of the Best Quality," reflecting its commitment to premium ingredients, consistent quality, and innovation. bb.q Chicken has rapidly expanded across North America and beyond, earning recognition as one of the fastest-growing restaurant chains in the U.S., as well as being named among Yelp's "Most Loved Brands."

About Macron

Macron is an iconic Italian brand in high-performance sportswear and a key player in an extraordinary growth journey over the past 20 years. Its success is rooted in an unwavering passion for innovative design, superior quality, and cutting-edge technology. By deeply understanding the needs and aspirations of teams, athletes, and sports enthusiasts, Macron consistently develops apparel and accessories that empower individuals to unlock their full potential, while fostering a strong sense of team spirit that inspires everyone to become the best version of themselves.

The name Macron originates from the Greek prefix "μακρο" (makro), meaning "great." This reflects the brand's core identity, where ambition and a commitment to continuous growth are fundamental values. The company's logo, the Macron Hero, represents an athlete celebrating the achievement of their goal and embodies the brand's philosophy: #BecomeYourOwnHero.

Macron's global leadership in rugby is firmly established through its partnerships at the highest level of the sport. The brand currently supplies three of the six national teams competing in the Six Nations Championship and has outfitted seven of the twenty teams at the most recent Rugby World Cup, in addition to key partnerships with leading rugby organizations including USA Rugby.
