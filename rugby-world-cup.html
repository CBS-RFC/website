THE BIGGEST MBA RUGBY EVENT IN AMERICA

The MBA Rugby World Cup brings together the best business school rugby clubs from across the globe for a single day of elite competition on the iconic Governor's Island in New York Harbor.

Columbia Business School's RFC is proud to host the 2026 edition — continuing a decades-long tradition of MBA rugby excellence. Whether you are a player, student, alumni, or supporter, come be part of history.

Group stage matches will run throughout the morning, culminating in knockout rounds and a championship final in the afternoon.
