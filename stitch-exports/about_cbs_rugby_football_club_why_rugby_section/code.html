WHY RUGBY?

The Ultimate Team Sport

Rugby is defined by an unbreakable bond that transcends the field. At CBS, it’s not just about the game; it’s about a global community of peers who share a commitment to collective success and mutual respect. The unique culture of rugby—built on social tradition and competitive fire—creates a network of friends and mentors that lasts a lifetime.

The thrill of the pitch mirrors the high-stakes environment of the MBA experience. It demands strategic thinking under pressure, physical resilience, and absolute trust in your teammates. Whether you are a seasoned veteran or picking up a ball for the first time, the RFC offers an outlet for leadership and grit that is unmatched by any other campus organization.

In the classroom, you learn the theory of leadership. On the pitch with CBS RFC, you live it. Our club brings together diverse backgrounds and industries, uniting us under one goal. It’s the perfect fit for the MBA journey—a place to push your limits, blow off steam, and forge the most meaningful connections of your graduate career.

OUR STORY

1968

THE FOUNDATION

The Columbia Business School Rugby Football Club was born from the grit and vision of MBA students seeking competition and camaraderie. Established in 1968, the club quickly became a pillar of the CBS social and athletic community.

1990s

EXPANDING THE PITCH

As the MBA landscape evolved, so did the RFC. The club expanded its tour schedule, competing against top-tier global business schools and hosting the inaugural regional tournaments that would define Ivy League rugby culture.

PRESENT

A GLOBAL TRADITION

Today, CBS RFC remains one of the most active clubs on campus. With a diverse roster of athletes from around the world, we continue to uphold the values of leadership, teamwork, and excellence both on and off the field.
